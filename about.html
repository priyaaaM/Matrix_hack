The Matrix (1999)

Director: The Wachowskis

Starring: Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss, Hugo Weaving

Genre: Science Fiction, Action

Released: March 31, 1999

Synopsis: Neo (Keanu Reeves) is a computer hacker who is drawn into a world of mystery when a group of rebels led by Morpheus (Laurence Fishburne) reveals that the reality he knows is actually a simulated construct created by sentient machines to pacify and subjugate the human population, while the real world is in ruins.

"What is real? How do you define real?"
